Keyboard Controls


w, a, s and d: Accelerate
q and e: Rotate left and right (yaw)
1 and 2: Rotate up and down (pitch)
3: Show 3D
z: Toggle Doppler effect, 3 state -- on, default, off.
x: Toggle absolute positions. Also 3 state.
c: Toggle visible positions. Also 3 state.
t: Toggle display time.
p: Toggle display positions.
- and =: Zoom in and out
[ and ]: Speedup, slowdown time
\: Reverse time
, and .: Zoom in and out in time axis on Minkowski diagram
space: Fire photon (rasteroids only).
h: Toggle this screen.
